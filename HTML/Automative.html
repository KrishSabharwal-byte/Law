Automotive and Electric Vehicles

Legal Solutions for the Future of Mobility

Our Automotive and Electric Vehicles (EVs) practice combines deep sectoral knowledge with strong regulatory expertise to support clients across the evolving mobility value chain. We advise leading global and domestic automotive manufacturers, component suppliers, EV start-ups, battery producers, fleet operators, financiers, and technology providers on the full spectrum of legal, commercial, and compliance issues in India’s automotive and clean mobility sector.

India’s transition toward electric and sustainable transport is being driven by regulatory initiatives, consumer demand, and technological innovation. Our team helps clients navigate this changing landscape by offering commercially pragmatic, legally robust, and industry-specific solutions.

Our Services include:

Regulatory and Compliance Advisory

  • Compliance with Central Motor Vehicles Act & Rules, Bharat Stage (BS) emission norms, Automotive Industry Standards (AIS), and EV-specific guidelines under the FAME India scheme.
  • Advising on licensing, homologation, safety standards, and environmental clearances.

Corporate and Commercial Transactions

  • Structuring and negotiation of joint ventures, strategic alliances, distributorships, and licensing agreements.
  • Cross-border M&A and investment transactions in automotive and EV sectors.

Electric Mobility Ecosystem

  • Advisory on charging infrastructure, battery swapping, battery recycling, and Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) compliance.
  • Structuring of public-private partnerships (PPPs) for EV adoption projects.

Financing and Investments

  • Structuring project finance and private equity/venture capital investment in manufacturing, leasing, and fleet electrification.
  • Advising on Production Linked Incentive (PLI) schemes and government subsidies.

Technology and IP

  • Licensing of EV technologies, mobility platforms, and fleet management software.
  • Protection of patents, designs, trademarks, and confidential know-how.

Litigation and Dispute Resolution

  • Representing clients in product liability, warranty claims, and recall matters.
  • Advising on disputes involving supply chain contracts, dealership agreements, and consumer complaints.

Why Choose Us

  • In-depth understanding of the automotive industry coupled with cross-border transaction experience.
  • Strong relationships with Indian regulators, state authorities, and industry associations.
  • Track record of assisting global brands in establishing and scaling operations in India.

With the convergence of automotive, energy, and technology sectors, we are uniquely positioned to guide clients in capturing growth opportunities while ensuring compliance with India’s complex regulatory regime.
