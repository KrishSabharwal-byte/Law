Banking and Finance Law Practice

India-Focused Advisory for Global and Domestic Clients

Our Banking and Finance practice is at the forefront of advising global and domestic clients on sophisticated financing transactions and regulatory matters involving India’s dynamic financial sector. With a deep understanding of Indian law and international financing norms, we regularly represent multinational banks, development finance institutions, NBFCs, fintech players, sponsors, and corporate borrowers.

We advise on the full spectrum of banking and finance transactions, including:

  • Syndicated Lending & Bilateral Loans
  • Acquisition Finance
  • Project & Infrastructure Finance
  • Trade & Asset Finance
  • Debt Restructuring & Refinancing
  • External Commercial Borrowings (ECBs)
  • Masala Bonds & INR-Denominated Bonds
  • Fintech, Payment Systems & Digital Lending
  • Regulatory Compliance under RBI, SEBI & FEMA
  • Insolvency and Debt Recovery (IBC, SARFAESI, DRT)

Our services include drafting and negotiating loan and security documentation, conducting due diligence, structuring transactions in compliance with Indian and cross-border regulations, and advising on enforcement and dispute strategies.
