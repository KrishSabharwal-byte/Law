Capital Markets

India’s capital markets have emerged as one of the most active and diverse in Asia, driven by robust investor participation, regulatory reforms, and a growing appetite for equity and debt financing. Our Capital Markets Practice offers strategic, end-to-end legal support to issuers, underwriters, investors, and intermediaries engaging in both domestic and cross-border securities offerings.

We advise on a broad range of transactions, including initial public offerings (IPOs), follow-on public offers (FPOs), qualified institutional placements (QIPs), rights issues, private placements, and listing of debt securities. Our expertise extends to offshore listings (GDRs, ADRs, Masala bonds), high-yield bonds, and structured finance products.

Our team has deep experience navigating the regulatory framework under SEBI regulations, the Companies Act, FEMA, stock exchange listing requirements, and other sector-specific compliance mandates. We assist clients through every stage—from structuring and due diligence to drafting offer documents, coordinating with regulators, and ensuring smooth execution of offerings.

We also advise on corporate governance requirements, continuous disclosure obligations, and insider trading compliance, as well as represent clients in regulatory investigations and enforcement proceedings before SEBI, stock exchanges, and other authorities.
