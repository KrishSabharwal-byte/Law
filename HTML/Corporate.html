Corporate Law Practice

India’s corporate sector is evolving rapidly, with expanding domestic enterprises, increasing foreign investment, and a dynamic regulatory landscape. Our Corporate Law Practice supports clients across industries in navigating this complexity with strategic, business-oriented legal advice that meets both local requirements and international standards.

We advise on the full spectrum of corporate and commercial matters, including:

  • Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A) – public and private transactions, asset purchases, share sales, amalgamations, and spin-offs.
  • Private Equity & Venture Capital – investments, exits, portfolio company support, and fund structuring.
  • Joint Ventures & Strategic Alliances – structuring, negotiation, and implementation for domestic and cross-border partnerships.
  • Corporate Restructuring & Reorganisation – insolvency-related restructuring, capital reduction, and internal reorganisations.
  • Corporate Governance & Compliance – board advisory, shareholder agreements, regulatory compliance under the Companies Act, SEBI, FEMA, and competition law.

We work closely with clients from deal inception to post-closing integration, offering pragmatic solutions backed by deep knowledge of India’s regulatory regime and commercial environment. Our cross-border expertise ensures that multinational corporations, investors, and Indian enterprises receive seamless, coordinated counsel across jurisdictions.

Our practice combines transactional strength with advisory depth, helping clients manage legal risks, capture growth opportunities, and achieve their strategic objectives in one of the world’s fastest-growing economies.
