Defence

Strategic Legal Solutions for India’s National Security and Aerospace Sectors

Our Defence and Aerospace practice offers comprehensive legal services to global defence contractors, domestic manufacturers, technology providers, and government entities operating in India’s highly regulated defence sector. With deep knowledge of India’s defence procurement policies and a proven record in cross-border transactions, we assist clients in navigating the legal, commercial, and compliance challenges unique to this industry.

India’s defence sector is undergoing rapid transformation, with a focus on indigenous manufacturing under the Atmanirbhar Bharat and Make in India initiatives, as well as increasing opportunities for foreign investment and technology transfer. Our team brings together multidisciplinary expertise in government contracting, international trade, intellectual property, financing, and dispute resolution to deliver end-to-end legal support.

Our Services include:

Regulatory and Compliance Advisory

  • Advising on the Defence Acquisition Procedure (DAP) and Defence Procurement Manual (DPM).
  • Licensing and industrial clearances under the Arms Act, 1959, and Industrial Development and Regulation Act.
  • Compliance with FDI norms, export controls, and offset obligations.

Government Contracts and Procurement

  • Assisting in bidding, tender preparation, and contract negotiations for supply of defence equipment and technology.
  • Structuring offset arrangements and joint ventures under DAP guidelines.

Technology Transfer and Licensing

  • Drafting and negotiating technology transfer, co-development, and co-production agreements.
  • Advising on intellectual property ownership and protection in collaborative projects.

Corporate and Transactional Support

  • Structuring mergers, acquisitions, and strategic alliances in the defence and aerospace sector.
  • Advising on private equity and venture capital investment in defence technology start-ups.

Dispute Resolution and Enforcement

  • Representing clients in contract disputes, arbitration, and enforcement proceedings related to defence contracts.
  • Advising on export/import restrictions, sanctions, and compliance investigations.

Why Choose Us

  • Sector-specific expertise combining international best practices with Indian regulatory insights.
  • Proven track record in sensitive, high-value defence transactions and disputes.
  • Trusted relationships with government bodies, industry leaders, and international contractors.

We understand that in the defence and aerospace sector, confidentiality, compliance, and precision are paramount. Our solutions are designed to help clients operate with legal certainty while achieving strategic and commercial goals.
