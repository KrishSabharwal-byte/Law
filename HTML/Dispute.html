Dispute Resolution & Arbitration Practice

India’s expanding business landscape has brought with it increasingly complex commercial disputes, often spanning multiple jurisdictions. Our Dispute Resolution & Arbitration Practice provides clients with strategic, commercially focused representation in contentious matters, combining deep knowledge of Indian law with the ability to operate seamlessly in cross-border contexts.

We represent multinational corporations, Indian enterprises, financial institutions, and investors in high-stakes disputes across a wide range of industries, including infrastructure, energy, manufacturing, technology, real estate, and financial services. Our expertise covers:

  • Domestic and International Arbitration – under institutional rules such as SIAC, LCIA, ICC, HKIAC, and UNCITRAL, as well as ad-hoc proceedings.
  • Commercial Litigation – before the Supreme Court of India, High Courts, and specialised tribunals, including the NCLT/NCLAT, DRT, and competition authorities.
  • Enforcement of Foreign Awards and Judgments – advising on recognition, execution, and associated litigation in India.
  • Regulatory & White-Collar Defence – representing clients in investigations and enforcement proceedings before SEBI, RBI, ED, CCI, and other agencies.
  • Mediation & Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) – facilitating negotiated settlements and early dispute management strategies.

We focus on minimising disruption to business operations while protecting our clients’ commercial and reputational interests. With strong advocacy skills, sector-specific insight, and a global network, we deliver results in contentious matters that demand precision, persistence, and strategic thinking.

Our cross-border capability ensures that clients benefit from international best practices, robust procedural knowledge, and solutions that align with both global standards and the nuances of Indian law.
