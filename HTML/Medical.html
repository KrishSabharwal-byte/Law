Medical Devices

Comprehensive Legal Support for India’s Growing MedTech Sector

Our Medical Devices practice delivers strategic legal solutions to global and domestic clients engaged in the manufacturing, import, distribution, and innovation of medical devices, diagnostics, and healthcare technologies. With India emerging as one of the fastest-growing MedTech markets, driven by regulatory reforms and increasing healthcare investment, we help clients navigate the sector’s evolving legal, compliance, and commercial landscape.

We act for multinational device manufacturers, start-ups, investors, distributors, and healthcare providers, offering advice that combines regulatory precision with practical business insight. Our team has extensive experience in cross-border transactions, product registration, clinical evaluations, and technology transfer within the medical device industry.

Our Services include:

Regulatory and Compliance

  • Advising on classification, registration, and licensing under the Medical Devices Rules, 2017 and Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940.
  • Assisting with Central Drugs Standard Control Organization (CDSCO) approvals for manufacturing, import, and sale.
  • Compliance with quality standards including ISO 13485, Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP), and Indian Pharmacopoeia standards.

Product Development and Commercialization

  • Drafting and negotiating manufacturing, supply, and distribution agreements.
  • Structuring R&D collaboration and co-development contracts.
  • Advising on pricing and marketing regulations, including compliance with the National Pharmaceutical Pricing Authority (NPPA).

Clinical Investigations and Trials

  • Regulatory advisory for conducting clinical investigations in India.
  • Preparing clinical trial agreements and informed consent documentation.
  • Liaising with ethics committees and ensuring compliance with Good Clinical Practice (GCP).

Corporate and Cross-Border Transactions

  • M&A, joint ventures, and strategic alliances in the MedTech sector.
  • Licensing and technology transfer agreements for medical devices and diagnostics.
  • Structuring investments and financing in compliance with FDI norms.

Intellectual Property

  • Protection and enforcement of patents, designs, and trademarks for medical devices.
  • Advising on regulatory data protection and trade secret safeguarding.

Litigation and Enforcement

  • Representing clients in product liability and consumer protection disputes.
  • Advisory on recalls, adverse event reporting, and regulatory enforcement actions

Why Choose Us

  • Specialist knowledge of India’s medical device regulations and market entry strategies.
  • Cross-border expertise in structuring deals, licensing, and technology collaborations.
  • Proven capability in handling both regulatory and contentious matters for high-risk devices.

We combine legal insight with sectoral expertise to help MedTech clients innovate, expand, and operate in India with confidence and compliance.
