Pharmaceuticals

Legal Solutions for India’s Dynamic Pharma Industry

Our Pharmaceuticals practice delivers comprehensive legal support to multinational and domestic companies across the pharmaceutical value chain—from research and development to manufacturing, marketing, and distribution. With India being a global leader in generic medicines and a key player in active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs), biosimilars, and contract manufacturing, we assist clients in navigating complex regulatory, transactional, and intellectual property challenges in this highly regulated sector.

We advise pharmaceutical companies, biotech firms, contract research and manufacturing organisations (CROs/CMOs), investors, and healthcare providers on both domestic and cross-border matters, combining deep industry knowledge with an understanding of global compliance standards.

Our Services include:

Regulatory and Compliance

  • Advising on licensing, manufacturing, and marketing approvals under the Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940 and allied rules.
  • Product registration and import/export approvals with the Central Drugs Standard Control Organization (CDSCO).
  • Compliance with Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP), pharmacovigilance obligations, and labelling requirements.

Corporate and Commercial Transactions

  • Structuring and negotiating mergers, acquisitions, joint ventures, and strategic alliances in the pharmaceutical sector.
  • Drafting distribution, supply, co-marketing, and co-promotion agreements.
  • Structuring contract manufacturing, technology transfer, and licensing arrangements.

Intellectual Property

  • Patents, trademarks, and trade secrets protection for pharmaceuticals, APIs, and formulations.
  • Representing clients in patent oppositions, infringement actions, and regulatory data protection matters.

Clinical Trials and Research

  • Advising on compliance with Indian Good Clinical Practice (GCP) guidelines and clinical trial regulations.
  • Structuring R&D collaboration agreements and investigator contracts.

Pricing and Market Access

  • Advising on National Pharmaceutical Pricing Authority (NPPA) regulations and price control compliance.
  • Structuring market entry strategies for new products in compliance with Indian laws.

Litigation and Dispute Resolution

  • Representing clients in regulatory enforcement actions, product liability claims, and commercial disputes.
  • Arbitration and litigation involving IP, licensing, and distribution arrangements.

Why Choose Us

  • Deep understanding of India’s pharmaceutical regulatory environment.
  • Experience in both generic and innovative drug market segments.
  • Cross-border expertise in structuring global supply, licensing, and R&D arrangements.

We partner with pharmaceutical clients to navigate India’s evolving market, safeguard innovation, and achieve commercial success while maintaining full compliance with local and international laws.
