Government & Public Sector Practice

India’s government and public sector play a pivotal role in shaping the country’s economic growth through large-scale infrastructure development, public procurement, public-private partnerships (PPPs), and policy-driven reforms. Our Government & Public Sector Practice advises domestic and international clients—ranging from multinational corporations to state-owned enterprises—on engaging effectively with government bodies, navigating regulatory requirements, and participating in public sector projects.

We assist clients with:

  • Public Procurement & Tender Advisory – bid preparation, compliance review, contract negotiation, and representation in bid challenges.
  • Public-Private Partnerships (PPPs) – structuring, documentation, and implementation in sectors such as transportation, energy, healthcare, and urban development.
  • Regulatory & Policy Advisory – interpreting and responding to government policies, legislative developments, and sector-specific regulations.
  • Government Contracts & Disputes – drafting, negotiation, and representation in contractual disputes, including before statutory tribunals and arbitral forums.
  • Compliance & Ethics – anti-bribery, anti-corruption, and public sector compliance programs in line with domestic law and global best practices.

With a deep understanding of India’s administrative and regulatory framework, we combine sector expertise with a solution-oriented approach, enabling clients to work seamlessly with central, state, and local authorities. Our cross-border experience ensures that foreign investors and contractors meet India’s legal standards while aligning with international governance and compliance requirements.

Our team’s experience in policy advocacy, legislative engagement, and dispute resolution positions us as a trusted advisor in projects that intersect the public and private spheres.
