White Collar Crime Practice in India

In today’s globalised and highly regulated business environment, white collar crime has emerged as one of the most significant legal challenges for corporates, senior executives, and financial institutions. Rapid economic growth, increasing cross-border transactions, and tightening regulatory oversight in India have elevated the importance of robust white collar crime defence and compliance strategies.

Our White Collar Crime Practice combines deep local knowledge of India’s regulatory and enforcement landscape with an international perspective, enabling us to advise and defend clients facing high-stakes investigations and prosecutions.

Our Expertise

We assist in every aspect of white collar crime matters, including:

  • Anti-Corruption & Anti-Bribery
    • Advisory and defence under the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 (POCA) and related statutes.
    • Compliance with the U.S. Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA) and UK Bribery Act for cross-border operations.
  • Corporate Fraud & Mismanagement
    • Representation in investigations by the Serious Fraud Investigation Office (SFIO) and Registrar of Companies (RoC).
    • Advisory on fraud prevention frameworks and whistleblower policies.
  • Money Laundering & Financial Crimes
    • Defence under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act, 2002 (PMLA).
    • Handling enforcement actions by the Enforcement Directorate (ED) and the Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU).
  • Securities & Market Offences
    • Representation in proceedings before the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI).
    • Advisory on insider trading, market manipulation, and disclosure violations.
  • Tax & Customs-Related Offences
    • Defence in prosecutions under the Income Tax Act and Customs Act.
    • Negotiation and settlement of disputes with tax and customs authorities.

Our Regulatory & Enforcement Bodies We Engage With

Our lawyers routinely appear before and liaise with:

  • Enforcement Directorate (ED)
  • Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI)
  • Serious Fraud Investigation Office (SFIO)
  • Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)
  • Directorate of Revenue Intelligence (DRI)

Our Approach

  • Rapid Response – Immediate crisis management to protect clients during raids, summons, and freezing orders.
  • Risk Mitigation – Proactive compliance audits to prevent violations.
  • Strategic Defence – Coordinated legal strategies for parallel criminal, regulatory, and civil proceedings.
  • Cross-Border Coordination – Collaboration with global offices to handle multi-jurisdictional investigations.

Recent Engagements

  • Represented a senior executive of a multinational bank in parallel ED and CBI proceedings for alleged money laundering.
  • Advised a Fortune 500 technology company on implementing FCPA-compliant anti-bribery controls in its Indian operations.
