E-Commerce

Strategic Legal Solutions for India’s Digital Marketplace

Our E-Commerce practice supports global and domestic businesses navigating the rapid evolution of India’s online retail and digital services ecosystem. With India’s e-commerce sector projected to be one of the fastest growing in the world, fuelled by increasing internet penetration, smartphone adoption, and digital payment solutions, companies face a dynamic regulatory and commercial environment.

We advise online marketplaces, direct-to-consumer (D2C) brands, social commerce platforms, fintech providers, logistics partners, investors, and technology companies on structuring operations, ensuring regulatory compliance, and mitigating legal risks in cross-border and domestic transactions.

Our approach blends sector-specific legal expertise with practical business insights, enabling clients to launch, scale, and protect their digital businesses while complying with Indian and international laws.

Our Services include:

Regulatory and Compliance Advisory

  • Advising on compliance with the Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020, Information Technology Act, 2000, and sector-specific regulations.
  • Advising on FDI compliance for multi-brand and single-brand e-commerce under the Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA).
  • Data privacy compliance under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and global data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA).

Corporate Structuring & Market Entry

  • Structuring cross-border and domestic e-commerce ventures.
  • Advising on optimal entity setup for foreign entrants, including liaison, branch, and subsidiary models.
  • Joint ventures, acquisitions, and strategic alliances in the e-commerce sector.

Commercial & Technology Contracts

  • Drafting and negotiating vendor, logistics, warehousing, and fulfilment agreements
  • Technology licensing, SaaS agreements, and payment gateway integration contracts.
  • Terms of service, privacy policies, and platform governance documentation.

Intellectual Property Protection

  • Trademark, copyright, and domain name protection strategies for digital brands.
  • Enforcement against online infringement, counterfeiting, and brand dilution.

Consumer Protection & Dispute Resolution

  • Advising on product liability, consumer complaints, and advertising regulations.
  • Representing clients in regulatory proceedings and online fraud disputes.

Taxation & Cross-Border Trade

  • Taxation & Cross-Border Trade
  • GST compliance for digital and physical goods.
  • Customs advisory for cross-border e-commerce shipments.

Why Choose Us

  • Cross-border e-commerce expertise with deep understanding of India’s FDI and technology laws.
  • Proven track record in resolving regulatory, consumer, and IP issues for global brands.
  • Integrated advisory across legal, commercial, and compliance needs for online businesses.

We help e-commerce innovators and established players navigate the complexities of India’s regulatory landscape while achieving seamless market expansion and sustainable growth.
