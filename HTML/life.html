Life Sciences

Legal Expertise Across Pharmaceuticals, Biotechnology, and Healthcare

Our Lifesciences practice provides end-to-end legal solutions to domestic and global companies in the pharmaceutical, biotechnology, medical devices, diagnostics, and healthcare sectors. With India being one of the world’s largest producers of generic medicines and a rapidly growing hub for clinical research and biotech innovation, we help clients navigate a complex regulatory landscape while advancing their commercial objectives.

We combine deep sector knowledge with cross-border transactional and compliance expertise, making us a trusted partner for multinational corporations, emerging biotech firms, research institutions, investors, and healthcare service providers.

Our Services include:

Regulatory and Compliance Advisory

  • Advising on compliance with the Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940, Medical Devices Rules, 2017, and allied healthcare regulations.
  • Assisting with product registration, licensing, and import/export approvals with the Central Drugs Standard Control Organization (CDSCO).
  • Compliance with clinical trial regulations, Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP), and pharmacovigilance norms.

Corporate and Commercial Transactions

  • Structuring and negotiating M&A, joint ventures, licensing, and distribution agreements in lifesciences and healthcare sectors.
  • Drafting technology transfer, R&D collaboration, and co-marketing arrangements.

Intellectual Property

  • Advising on patents, trademarks, designs, and regulatory data protection for pharmaceuticals and biotech innovations.
  • Representing clients in IP enforcement, oppositions, and infringement actions.

Clinical Trials and Research

  • Structuring clinical trial agreements and investigator contracts in compliance with Indian Good Clinical Practice (GCP) guidelines.
  • Advising on ethical approvals and patient consent documentation.

Healthcare and MedTech

  • Regulatory advice for digital health platforms, telemedicine, and AI-based diagnostics.
  • Compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 for handling health data.

Litigation and Dispute Resolution

  • Representing clients in product liability claims, pricing disputes, and regulatory enforcement actions.
  • Arbitration and court proceedings involving licensing, supply chain, and R&D disputes.

Why Choose Us

  • Deep understanding of India’s lifesciences regulations and global industry standards.
  • Strong track record in cross-border transactions and regulatory approvals.

With the lifesciences sector driving healthcare innovation and global supply chains, our firm is committed to helping clients achieve compliance, safeguard innovation, and succeed in India’s fast-evolving market.
